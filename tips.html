Tips for Clean Air

Simple steps you can take to help reduce air pollution and improve air quality for everyone.

Home | About Air Pollution | Blog | Contact | Tips for Clean Air

Everyday Clean Air Tips

  • Use public transportation or carpool to reduce vehicle emissions.
  • Limit use of fireplaces and wood stoves to reduce particulate pollution.
  • Plant trees to naturally filter air pollutants and improve oxygen levels.
  • Support clean energy initiatives in your community.

Simple Changes in Daily Life

  • Opt for energy-efficient appliances and reduce electricity consumption.
  • Reduce waste by recycling, composting, and avoiding single-use plastics.
  • Engage with local air quality programs to help monitor and improve air in your area.
